Casein and Coproducts

concepts
casein

Traditionally casein was separated from milk by precipitation. There are two broad approaches to precipitation:

  • Chemical: isolectric precipitation using acid
  • Biochemical: enzymatic precipitation using rennet

More recently physical methods of separation were introduced, typically relying on membranes. One advantage of physical methods is that they do not generate an insoluble material.
